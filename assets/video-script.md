## 📹 RECORDING TIPS

### Good Lighting
- Record in daylight or well-lit room
- Avoid backlighting

### Clear Audio
- Use headphones with mic
- Record in quiet room
- Speak clearly, not too fast

### Engaging Visuals
- Show your face briefly (not required)
- Highlight key information
- Use gestures

### Call to Action
- Always ask for likes/comments
- Tell people what to do next
